Moody Beach Maine - The Canonicus

The Canonicus is our summer oceanfront rental property located on Moody Beach (private beach), Wells, Maine. This 3 bedroom, 1.5 bath house is strategically located halfway between two of the few public ways on Moody, ensuring a private beach experience.

Canonicus (pronounced kuh-NON-ih-kus) is named after a Narragansett sachem (chief) who was a prominent leader in the early 17th century and maintained peaceful relations with English settlers in the region.

  • Stove, microwave, refrigerator, and Keurig coffee machine
  • Clothes washer, dryer, and second refrigerator in basement
