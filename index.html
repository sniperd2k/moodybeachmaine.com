Moody Beach Maine - The Canonicus

The Canonicus is my summer oceanfront rental property located on Moody Beach (private beach), Wells, Maine. It is a 3 bedroom, 1.5 bath house. It is strategically located halfway between two of the few public ways on Moody, thus ensuring a private beach experience!

Rental Information

  • Maximum of 6 occupants, including babies and children of all ages
  • No pets of any kind
  • Clothes washer and dryer are in the basement
  • Free high speed Internet/Wifi
  • Guests are expected to leave the house as clean as they have found it
  • Check out is on Saturday at 11:00 am, check in is at 12:30 pm

If you have an electric vehicle (Tesla, Nissan Leaf, etc.) a NEMA 14-50 outdoor outlet is availble for your use!

Rental is available from June until October and is typically in 2 week blocks. Please contact me for availability and pricing.
